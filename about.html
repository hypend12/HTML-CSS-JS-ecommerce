Hawaiian tradition and the Aloha Shirt

Who we are ?

In the 1930's and 1940's the "Aloha Shirt" became a universal symbol of Hawaii's warmth and spirit. Today, almost 90 years later, we are carrying on the Hawaiian tradition by manufacturing
the highest quality, most authentic versions of the islands favorite designs. From the warm, romantic days of old Hawaii,
we proudly present our collection of "Made in Hawaii", the islands most authentic designs of nostalgic and modern Aloha shirts and dresses, all of which embody the spirit of Aloha!
